Task 1 - Instructions

In this task, you will interact with one other person in the room, chosen at random.

One participant will be chosen to play Role A and the other will be chosen to play Role B.

At the end of the task, any points you have earned will be converted to dollars, at the rate of $2 per point.

At the beginning of the task, the Role A participant is given 4 points. The Role A participant may decide to keep all those points, or send some or all of them to the Role 2 participant.

Every point sent to the Role 2 participant will then be multiplied by three.

After Participant A has made his or her decision, the points are multiplied, and Participant B can choose to send some of the multiplied points back to Participant A.

Participant B can send back all of the points, none of the points, or any amount in between.

Note, Participant B will be asked to enter his/her decisions in advance – indicating for each possible amount transferred, how much he/she will send back. We will then calculate the outcome using the answer that is relevant, given Participant Aʼs actual choice.
