Instructions for Participant B:

On the next page you will be shown eighteen of your originally chosen statements from Task 2, together with the prices you named for switching those statements

We will use a random process to determine which of these answers will be revealed to your interaction partner.

First indicate by selecting all answers that you would prefer to conceal from your interaction partner, if you could select as many as you wish.

Rank the statements you wish to conceal in order from most wish to conceal to least wish to conceal.

You do not need to nominate any answers to be concealed. If you deﬁnitely want to reveal an answer, then do not rank it at all.

Make sure that you enter a complete ranking such as (1, 2, 3, 4). If you leave any gaps (1, 2, 4, 5), or have any duplicates (1, 2, 2, 3) your answer will be invalid and you won’t be able to proceed.

Note that there is a time limit on this task: you will have 3 minutes and 30 seconds to enter your ranking. If you do not complete your ranking in this time, all your statements will be revealed.
