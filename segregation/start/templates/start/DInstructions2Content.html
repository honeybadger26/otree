Decision on deduction points

From period 4 onwards, you can decide whether to decrease the Payoff of each other inhabitant of the town you have chosen by assigning deduction points. The other inhabitants in your town can also decrease your Payoff if they wish to. Deductions can only be applied within a town, i.e. you cannot decrease the Payoff of an inhabitant of another town, and inhabitants of the other town cannot decrease your Payoff.

The deduction decision screen looks as follows.



Each deduction point that you assign to another inhabitant costs you 1 point and will reduce his or her Payoff by 3 points. If you choose to decrease the Payoff of any inhabitant of your town, you must enter the number of deduction points you wish to assign in the box corresponding to that inhabitant. If you do not wish to change the Payoff of a specific inhabitant then you must enter 0. You can assign up to 10 points to each inhabitant.

For example:

  • If you assign 0 deduction points to a particular inhabitant, this decision will not alter either his/her Payoff or your own Payoff;
  • If you assign 1 deduction point to an inhabitant, this decision will decrease the Payoff of this inhabitant by 3 points and your own Payoff by 1 point;
  • If you assign 2 deduction points, this decision will decrease the inhabitant's Payoff by 6 points and your own Payoff by 2 points;
  • and so on.

Similarly, each decision point assigned to you by another inhabitant will reduce your Payoff by 3 points. How much the Payoff is decreased depends on the sum of deduction points received.

For instance:

  • If somebody receives a total of 3 deduction points (from all other inhabitants of the town in this period), his or her Payoff would be decreased by 9 points;
  • If somebody receives a total of 4 deduction points, his or her Payoff is reduced by 12 points.

Your total payoff at the end of a period is therefore calculated as follows:

Payoff - 3 x (sum of received deduction points) - sum of deduction points you have assigned

It is possible that your total Payoff can be reduced below zero. If this happens your losses will be deducted from your 600 participation points, but will not affect your {{ session.config.participation_fee }} show up payment.

After you have assigned points to each of the other inhabitants you must click the button "calculate". On the screen, you will then see the total costs of your assigned points. As long as you have not yet clicked the OK button, you can still change your decision. To recalculate the costs after a change of your assigned points, simply press the "calculate" button again.

You will have a limited time to make your decision. If you do not make a decision within the time limit, you will assign zero deduction points to all other residents in your town.
