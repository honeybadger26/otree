If you have a question at any time, please raise your hand and the monitor will come to your desk to answer it. When you ask a question, please make sure to speak softly so as not to disturb the other participants.

During the experiment, you can earn "points". At the end of the experiment these points will be converted to cash at the following rate:

1 point = ${{ session.config.real_world_currency_per_point }}

All the decisions will be anonymous. Each participant will be assigned a different ID number that will be shown on his/her computer screen.

Your earnings in Task 1 will not have an effect on your earnings in Task 2. That is, nothing you do in Task 2 can change your Task 1 earnings.

At the end of the experiment, we will randomly choose either Task 1 or Task 2. You will be paid your show up payment of {{ session.config.participation_fee }}, plus your earnings from whichever one of these tasks is chosen.
