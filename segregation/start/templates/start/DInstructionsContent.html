Decision on which town to move into

At the beginning of the task, Red individuals reside in Red Town and Blue individuals reside in Blue Town.

For the first 3 periods, you will be asked to confirm that you are living in your starting town, and no other choice will be possible.

After period 3, at the beginning of each period each participant decides whether to stay in their current town, or to move to the other town. You can move to either town, regardless of whether you are a member of the Red group or the Blue group. The decision screen looks as follows.



Red individuals earn an additional 50 points per period. Blue individuals earn an additional 150 points per period. After all participants have chosen their town, the combined earnings of all the residents are divided equally among the inhabitants of the respective town.

In periods 1 to 3, then, the four residents of Red town will earn a total of 200 points per period, which will be shared equally between the four of them: 50 points each per period.

In these same periods, the four residents of Blue town will earn a total of 600 points per period, which will be shared equally: 150 points each per period.

To illustrate the effect of moving town, suppose that in period 4, one red individual decides to move to Blue town, while everyone else stays in the same place. The table below shows that the residents of Red Town will earn 50 points each, while the residents of Blue Town will earn 130 points each.

Red Town Blue Town
Residents 3 x Red, 0 x Blue 1 x Red, 4 x Blue
Total Earnings 3 x 50 = 150 1 x 50 + 4 x 150 = 650
Earnings per resident 150 / 3 = 50 650 / 5 = 130

Feedback

After all participants have made their decisions, the following Payoff screen will show you who has moved into the town you are in and how much each participant has earned.



After the first period, a "history" display will be made available on the screen for you to view the past decisions of all the participants. You may consult this history display when making your subsequent decisions.

Time to make decision

Each period you will have a limited time to make your decision. If you do not complete your decision within the time limit, the computer will make a random decision on your behalf.
