Stage 2: The green button

This stage arises when, on the previous stage, one participant pressed the red button, and another participant had their endowment reduced.

In this stage, two of the other three participants will be chosen at random to have the opportunity to press a green button. These participants will be given 40 additional ECU each, prior to making a decision whether to press the button.

The green button does the “reverse” of the red button. If at least one of the two individuals presses the button, it will:

  • reduce the endowment of both green button individuals by 40 ECU, and
  • increase the endowment of whoever lost ECU in the previous stage by 120 ECU, returning them to 200 ECU endowment.

To illustrate, suppose that persons B and D are one chosen to have the opportunity to press the green button.

If neither B nor D presses the button, then each of them will keep their additional 40 ECU, and all the other earnings from the earlier stage will be unchanged.

A B C D E
80 240 240 240 200

If at least one of B or D chooses to press the button, the earnings will be:

A B C D E
200 200 240 200 200

After the decision has been made whether or not to press the green button, participants enter the final stage of the round, Stage 3.
