You are in a group of five people. You will be in the same group for 20 rounds. Each round, you will be randomly assigned a new identifier (A, B, C, D, E), so you won ºt be able to tell who is whom from round to round.

Each round, everyone is endowed with 200 ECU (economic currency units).
