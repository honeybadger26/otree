Task 3 - Part B Instructions

You will be presented with each of the statements you accepted in Part A.

For each statement, you will be asked whether you wish to enter a bid between $1 and $100, for which you would be willing to accept the opposite statement.

If you do not wish to enter a bid, type "out".

After all your bids are entered, we will select one statement at random, and generate a random number between 1–100.

  • If the number generated is greater than or equal to your bid for that statement, we will pay you the randomly generated number (in dollars) in exchange for changing your statement.
  • If the number is less than your bid, or if you opted out, we will pay you nothing, and your statement will be unchanged.

So for example: if we roll 93, and you nominated $80, we will pay you $93 to accept the opposite statement. If we roll 74, the number will be below your nominated price, so you will be paid nothing, and you won’t change which statement you accept.

At the end of the Task, we will ask you to sign a printed copy of the statement you have accepted, as a condition of payment.
