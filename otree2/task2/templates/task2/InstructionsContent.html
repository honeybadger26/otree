Task 2 - Instructions

In this task, you will interact with one other person in the room, chosen at random. This person will definitely NOT be the same person you interacted with in any of the earlier tasks.

One participant will be chosen to play Role 1 and the other will be chosen to play Role 2. The task will have six rounds. You will play the same role for all six rounds.

At the end of the task, any points you have earned will be converted to dollars, at the rate of 1 point per dollar. You will be paid your earnings for all rounds.

In each round, the Role 1 participant is given 2 points. The Role 1 participant may decide to keep all those points, or send some or all of them to the Role 2 participant.

Every point sent to the Role 2 participant will then be multiplied by three.

After Participant 1 has made his or her decision, the points are multiplied, and Participant 2 can choose to send any amount (including none or all) of the multiplied points back to Participant 1.
