Stage 1: The red button

Two of the {{ session.config.num_demo_participants }} members of your group will be randomly selected to have the opportunity to press a red button.

If only one person presses their red buttons, this person will earn an additional 40 ECU. No one else will be affected.

If both participants choose to press their red buttons, it will have two effects:

  • The participants who press the red buttons will earn an additional 20 ECU each.
  • One other person in the group, chosen at random, will lose 120 ECU from their endowment.

If no one presses their red button, nothing happens, and the round is over.

To illustrate, suppose that persons C and E are chosen at random to have the opportunity to press the red button, and person A is the person who will be affected if the red button is pressed.

If both C and E press the button, the effects will be:

A B C D E
-120 0 +20 0 +20

If only C presses the red button, the effects will be:

A B C D E
0 0 +40 0 0

If neither C nor E presses their red button, the round is over and you will start a new round.

If either C or E presses the button, the round enters an additional stage (either Stage 2 or Stage 3).
