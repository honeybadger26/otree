Stage 1: The red button

One of the {{ session.config.num_demo_participants }} members of your group will be randomly selected to have the opportunity to press a red button.

If the red button is pressed, it will have two effects:

  • Whoever presses the button will earn 40 ECU.
  • One other person in the group, chosen at random, will lose 120 ECU from their endowment.

To illustrate, suppose that person C is chosen at random to have the opportunity to press the red button, and person A is the person who will be affected if the red button is pressed.

If C does not press the button, everyone's earnings will be unchanged.

If C does press the button, the effects will become:

A B C D E
-120 0 +40 0 0

If C does not press the button, the round is over and you will start a new round.

If C does press the button, you will enter the next stage.
